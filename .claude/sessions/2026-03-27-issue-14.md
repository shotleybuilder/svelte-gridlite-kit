## Notes
- Race condition: Svelte reactive updates are batched, so `validGrouping` is stale when `rebuildGroupedQuery()` runs synchronously after `setGrouping([])`
- After first `await`, reactivity runs and `validGrouping` becomes `[]`, causing `validGrouping[0]` to be undefined
- Issue recommends Option B (snapshot) as safest approach
